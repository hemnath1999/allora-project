About Allora

Allora is a cultural fest that is conducted by SNS College Of Technolgy, Coimbatore. This carnival came into the picture on 2010 launched by our college's chairman Dr. S N Subramanian. The main motto of this cultural fest is to bring the best out of the students who longe for an opportunity to prove themselves in a big stage! Fear is the main enemy for an individual. So, to eradicate it we have prepared a wonderful platform fo the students. This cultural fest will not only make you to participate in your favourite events but will help in finding your own self!
